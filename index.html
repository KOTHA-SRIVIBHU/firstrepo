The Wise Owl and the Curious Squirrel

In a forest, Sammy the squirrel loved to explore and ask questions. One day, he asked Oliver the wise owl, Where do the stars go during the day?

Oliver smiled. The stars are always there, Sammy. The sun s light just hides them from our view.

Sammy thought for a moment and asked, Why do flowers only bloom in spring?

Oliver replied, Because spring brings the warmth and rain they need. Everything has its time, Sammy.

Sammy kept learning from Oliver and soon realized wisdom is about more than answers it s about appreciating life s wonders.
